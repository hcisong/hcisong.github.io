Headset Configuration for the User Study!

Thank you for your interest in participating in this user study. To get started, please follow these instructions:

Hardware Required: Meta Quest 1 or Meta Quest 2 or Meta Quest Pro
Estimated Time: Approximately 45 minutes

Manual in VR

Getting Started:

  • Step 1: Put on your headset.

  • Step 2: Ensure that your Wi-Fi settings are correctly configured to ensure internet access.

  • Step 3: From the main menu, locate and select the "Apps" icon.

  • Step 4: Inside the "Apps" section, you will find the "Browser" application.

  • Step 5: Open the "Browser" to access the internet.

  • Step 6: In the browser's address bar, type "hcisong.github.io" and press Enter. Look for the link titled "LINK TO NEW USER STUDY: SVS" and click on it. Alternatively, you can directly enter "hcisong.github.io/svs/index.html".

  • Step 7: For a more immersive experience with the virtual objects, click on the headset icon situated in the bottom right corner of the browser. This will fully immerse you in the virtual environment.

  • Step 8: In the virtual environment, to rotate the model, point the controller at it. You'll see a line connecting to the model. Once you press the trigger on the controller and the point turns blue, the model will begin to rotate. [Note: Some models are not rotatable.]

  • Step 9: After observing the object for at least one minute, press the virtual button in the virtual environment to complete the questionnaire. Alternatively, press the menu button on the controller to exit VR mode and open a new questionnaire tab.

You are now ready to actively participate in the user study.
